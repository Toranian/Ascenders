About Ascenders

The Ascenders Climbing Gym is a not-for-profit society in Vancouver, BC, with the mandate of making
climbing accessing for everyone, regardless of ability or ability to pay.  With over 15,000 square
feet (1,400 square metres) of climbing space.  The Ascenders Climbing Gym is a community facility 
and a unique social organization, providing adaptive recreation and rehabilitation programs, youth
recreational and competitive programs, as well as providing a climbing academy at select local secondary
schools in the region. All instructors are certified by the Association of Canadian Mountain Guides.
